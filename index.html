About Me

Hi there! You've stumbled upon my website where I keep all the good stuff I make to trick people into wanting to work with me. I'm a full-stack web developer, so I keep a tech blog on cool things I find on the interwebs and my learning experiences I gain through working with people, going to meet-ups, wrestling with new tech, etc. You can also check out my projects here and on Github.

In my free time, I also do some experimental art and music. Just like I love learning new things and applying them in web development, I do the same thing with art and music. Recently, I've been playing around with different mediums. I used to be a pencil/pen and paper person, but I've expanded to using chalk and paint on cardboard, wood, etc. It's been really fun finding out what you can do with different materials.

For music, it's the same way. I have a little more background with music as my art has been mainly an independent journey, but I have many talented friends who have helped me navigate the world of audio processing and hardware. I started out with just a USB mic, an acoustic guitar, and way too much self-consciousness about recording music. I've since then gotten over that and have expanded my music explorations to DJ-ing, mixing, producing, and recording music in a more analytical way.
